How to Build a Sound Proof Room


In this increasingly busy, noisy world, we all need a little peace and quiet, especially in our own homes. Soundproofing a room can help block out the din of traffic, sirens, airplanes, honking horns and other outdoor noises. And if the noise is coming from within a room, such as a loud sound system, television, or band practice, soundproofing can keep the racket from disrupting your home life.Here are several ways to soundproof a room, including some super-simple tips and more-advanced techniques, which include sound-absorbing products.


Any piece of equipment that shakes, rattles, or rolls produces vibrations that can transfer noise to other rooms. This includes items such as, speakers, appliances, and exercise gear. To dampen the vibrations and quiet the room, set the item on a thick piece of dense foam.
